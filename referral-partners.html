The Process

What happens when you refer a client

01

You make the introduction

A simple email, call, or text. "I want to introduce you to a broker I trust." That's all we need. We handle all follow-up professionally.

02

Confidential consultation

We meet with your client under NDA, learn about their business and goals, and give an honest assessment of their options — including whether now is the right time.

03

Free written valuation

If they'd like to move forward, we prepare a full written valuation. If they decide not to sell, they keep the valuation at no cost. Zero pressure.

04

Confidential sale & close

We handle marketing, buyer screening, negotiation, and closing. Your client's business is handled with complete professionalism from start to finish.

Our Commitment to You

We protect your relationship

Your long-term relationship with your client is worth more than any single transaction. We operate with that understanding in every interaction.

We never poach

We do not market other services to your client or pitch them anything beyond the transaction they were referred for. Your client relationship stays yours.

We keep you informed

With your client's permission, we provide you appropriate updates on the transaction. No surprises, no discoveries after the fact.

We reciprocate referrals

When our clients need accounting, legal, financial, or banking services, we refer them to professionals in our network — including you, if you'd like to be on that list.
