Our Story

A different kind
of broker.

County South was founded in 1989 with a straightforward mission: help business owners in the Carolinas exit with dignity, discretion, and maximum value.

We are not a national franchise or a volume shop. We are a locally owned, independent brokerage with deep roots in the NC and SC business communities.

Our brokers have personally operated businesses. They understand what it means to have built something from nothing — and what it takes to pass it on to the right hands.

"Most of our sellers are doing this for the first and only time in their lives. They deserve someone in their corner who treats it that way."

We specialize in the mid-market: $100,000 to $5,000,000. Our sweet spot is where most national platforms fall short — too large for classified sites, too small for investment bankers.
