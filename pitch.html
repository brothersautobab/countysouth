Key Findings

What's holding the site back

Three critical issues and five high-priority improvements. None of these are hard to fix — they just haven't been addressed yet.

Critical

The listing page is broken on mobile

The listings use an HTML table with 178+ empty columns. On a smartphone — where 60%+ of web traffic now comes from — the listings page is completely unreadable. Buyers give up and look elsewhere.

Critical

No hero, no hook, no reason to stay

The homepage opens straight into three sentences of boilerplate. There's no visual moment, no clear headline, and no immediate answer to "why should I work with County South?" A seller evaluating brokers leaves in under 10 seconds.

Critical

7 pages are hidden from the navigation

Pages for Buyers & Sellers, Business Resources, and Insurance exist but only appear in one footer — buried below the listings. Most visitors never find them. These pages represent real value that's being wasted.

High Priority

No social proof anywhere on the site

35 years of closed deals. Hundreds of satisfied clients. But the site shows none of it — no testimonials, no transaction count, no team photos. In brokerage, trust is the product. The site doesn't sell it.

High Priority

Buyers and sellers need different journeys

A business owner thinking about selling has completely different questions than a buyer browsing listings. Right now they both land on the same generic homepage with no clear direction for either.

Quick Win

Typo on a primary CTA: "Sell My Businesss"

Three S's in "Businesss" on a call-to-action button. Small detail, but on a site representing multi-million dollar transactions, polish signals professionalism — and its absence signals the opposite.

What Was Built

Five fully designed pages

Every page is complete, interactive, and live at the demo link above. Here's what each one does.

Home

Hero with dual CTAs, trust signals, stats bar, quick search, preview listings, 6-step process, testimonial, and seller CTA. Sets the tone immediately.

Listings

Live from Supabase database. Filter by type, sort by price. Each listing slides up a full detail sheet with NDA request form built in.

Full seller landing page: promises, 6-step process timeline, free valuation form, and FAQ accordion with the most common seller questions answered.

Company story, 4 core values, company timeline since 1989, 6 specialty sectors, full team cards with bios, and IBBA/NCBA/BBB affiliations.

Office location, email, and social. Full contact form with interest selector. Clean, direct, and confidentiality-forward.
