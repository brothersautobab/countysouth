Seller FAQ

How do you keep my sale confidential? +

We never list your business name or location publicly. Every buyer signs an NDA before receiving any details. You approve who gets information. Your employees, customers, and competitors won't know until you're ready.

What does it cost to list with you? +

Zero upfront. We are compensated only at closing as a percentage of the sale price. Our interests are perfectly aligned with yours — we earn more when you earn more.

How long does it take? +

Most businesses close within 6–12 months. Businesses priced at market value with organized financials typically sell faster. We'll give you a realistic timeline in our first conversation.

I just want to know what it's worth — no commitment. +

That's exactly where most sellers start. A valuation is free, confidential, and carries zero obligation. The number is yours to do with as you wish — including deciding not to sell. Fill out the form above and we'll be in touch within one business day.
